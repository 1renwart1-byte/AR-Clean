# Privacy Policy for AR Clean

**Last Updated: May 23, 2024**

AR Clean ("the App") is developed and operated by **ARH Community** ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

By using AR Clean, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### A. Personal Information (User-Provided)
To provide the best experience and manage premium features, we may collect:
*   **Account Information:** When you sign in via Google (Firebase Authentication), we receive your **name**, **email address**, and **profile picture**.
*   **Purpose:** This data is used solely for user identification, synchronizing your Premium subscription status across devices, and providing customer support.

### B. Device and Technical Information (Automated)
We automatically collect certain information about your device:
*   **Identifiers:** Unique device ID (Android ID), Google Advertising ID (GAID), and Firebase Installation ID.
*   **Technical Data:** Device model, hardware specifications, OS version, system language, and time zone.
*   **Performance Data:** Crash logs and performance metrics via **Firebase Crashlytics** and **Firebase Analytics**.
*   **Purpose:** To analyze app stability, optimize performance for specific hardware, and for advertising attribution.

### C. Financial Information
*   **Transaction Data:** We collect transaction IDs and subscription status via **Google Play Billing**.
*   **Note:** We do **NOT** collect or store credit card details or bank account information. All payments are processed securely by Google Play.

## 2. Disclosure of Sensitive Permissions

AR Clean requires specific high-level permissions to fulfill its core purpose as a utility tool. We adhere to the principle of data minimization:

### A. All Files Access (`MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`)
*   **Core Functionality:** This permission is essential for the **Junk Clean**, **Large File Finder**, **WhatsApp Cleaner**, and **Duplicate Detection** features.
*   **How it's used:** It allows the App to scan the entire device storage to identify redundant, temporary, and large files for deletion.
*   **Privacy Guarantee:** Scanning is performed **locally on your device**. We do not read, modify, or upload your personal documents, photos, or private files to any server.

### B. All Packages Visibility (`QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES`)
*   **Core Functionality:** This permission is required for the **App Manager** and **Security Risk Scan**.
*   **How it's used:** It allows the App to list all installed applications to identify high-risk software (e.g., malware or modified apps), manage background processes, and calculate total storage usage accurately.
*   **Compliance:** This data is used only for the purpose of app management initiated by the user.

### C. Usage Statistics (`PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS`)
*   **Core Functionality:** Used by the **RAM Boost** and **Device Performance Monitoring** features.
*   **How it's used:** It allows the App to detect which applications are currently consuming system resources (CPU/RAM) in real-time.
*   **Privacy Guarantee:** This information is used only to show you resource consumption and is not stored or shared.

### D. Media Permissions (Android 13+)
*   **Permissions:** `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`, `READ_MEDIA_VIDEO`, `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO`.
*   **Purpose:** Required on newer Android versions to categorize media files so you can specifically manage and delete large videos or images.

## 3. Data Processing and Security

*   **Local Processing:** 99% of our app's analysis (scanning for junk, identifying large files) happens locally on your device.

*   **No Data Selling:** We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information or device data to third-party data brokers.
