## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
We collect certain personal information to provide and improve our services:

Our App offers Premium features via Google Play Billing.
*   **Purchase History:** We collect transaction IDs and subscription status.

We use Firebase and other tools to collect technical data for stability and performance:

*   **Performance Metrics:** Crash logs, ANR reports, and app usage patterns via **Firebase Crashlytics**.
*   **Advertising ID:** We may collect the Google Advertising ID (GAID) for attribution and advertising purposes. You can reset or opt-out of this via your Android device settings.

## 2. Sensitive Permissions & Data Usage

AR Clean is a utility tool that requires specific permissions to perform its core functions. We follow the principle of data minimization and use these permissions strictly for the described purposes:

### A. Full Storage Access (`MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`)
*   **Purpose:** Required for the **Junk Clean**, **Large Files**, and **Duplicate Detection** features. This allows the App to scan the device storage to identify and delete redundant files that occupy space.
*   **User Privacy:** Scanning is performed **locally on your device**. We do **not** read, modify, or upload your personal documents, photos, or videos to any external server.

### B. All Packages Visibility (`QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES`)
*   **Purpose:** This is a **Core Functionality** for the **App Manager** and **Security Scan** features. It allows the App to list all installed applications to detect high-risk software, identify unused apps, and manage background processes.
*   **Compliance:** This permission is used only for user-initiated cleaning and management functions.

### C. Usage Statistics (`PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS`)
*   **Purpose:** Used by the **Boost Speed** and **Ram Boost** features to identify which applications are consuming system resources (RAM and CPU) in real-time, allowing the user to optimize performance.

### D. Media Permissions (Android 13+)
*   **Permissions:** `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`, `READ_MEDIA_VIDEO`, `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO`.
*   **Purpose:** Used on newer Android versions to categorize media files for the user to manage and delete if necessary.
